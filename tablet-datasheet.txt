Tablet is little endian

Tablet has three USB interfaces

Button input packets start with a 06 byte (as apposed to 07 for Pen input).
The 2nd and 3rd bytes are the button codes.

Button Codes: (Hex)
1: 00 05
2: 00 08
3: 04 00
4: 00 2c
5: 00 19
6: 01 16
7: 01 1d
8: 05 11
9: 01 57
10: 01 56

Because each of the second bytes in the button codes are unique, the first byte can be ignored for ease of implementation.
